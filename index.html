About Me

Hi! Welcome to my portfolio! I am Shiv Jhalani, a junior Computer Science major, with a math minor and keen focus on backend development alongside an evolving interest in data science.

My passion for coding ignited in 2017, sparked by a Java course that not only taught me about loops and classes but also unveiled my true calling in computer science. Since then, coding has been an incredibly impactful part of my life, shaping my interests and my path forward.

On this path, I am learning how much value lies in the teamwork aspect of coding; I tend to thrive in environments where ideas are shared, where challenges are tackled collectively, and where innovative solutions emerge from diverse perspectives. Aiming to extend this collaborative spirit beyond my professional endeavors, I actively seek out opportunities to engage with and learn from others in all walks of life.

What keeps me going in this ever evolving field is the thrill of learning something new every day: the buzz of fixing a pesky bug and the satisfaction of seeing my code come to life. I'm constantly on a quest to add more tools to my tech toolbox, be it through hackathons, research projects, or just playing around with some cool APIs I found!

Outside the world of brackets and braces, you'll find me exploring hiking trails, hitting the gym with the newest fitness challenge, geeking out over the latest tech gadgets, or diving into a good book. I stringly believe that a balanced life fuels creativity and keeps one's passion burning bright.

Welcome to my corner of the internet! Dive into my portfolio, to see projects and skills, but more importantly, snippets of my journey and passion in this field. I love meeting people so let’s connect!
