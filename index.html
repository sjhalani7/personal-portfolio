About Me

Hi! Welcome to my portfolio. My name is Shiv Jhalani, and I am a Junior Computer Science major, with a math minor and a keen focus on backend development alongside an evolving interest in data science.

My passion for coding ignited in 2017, sparked by a Java course that not only taught me about loops and classes but also unveiled my true calling in computer science. Since then, coding has been more than a pursuit—it's my playground, my challenge, and my art.

But something I learned really quickly is how much I valued the teamwork aspect of coding. I realized that I thrive in environments where ideas are shared, challenges are tackled collectively, and innovative solutions emerge from diverse perspectives; I am to extend this collaborative spirit beyond my professional endeavors, as I actively seek out opportunities to engage with and learn from others in all walks of life.

What keeps me going in this ever evolving field is the thrill of learning something new every day, the buzz of overcoming a tough challenge, and the satisfaction of seeing my code come to life. I'm constantly on a quest to add more tools to my tech toolbox, be it through late-night coding sessions, hackathons, research projects, or just some cool APIs I wanted to play around with!

Outside the world of brackets and braces, you'll find me exploring hiking trails, hitting the gym with the newest fitness challenge, geeking out over the latest tech gadgets, or diving into a good book. I believe that a balanced life fuels creativity and keeps one's passion burning bright.

So, welcome to my corner of the internet! Dive into my portfolio, where you'll see not just projects and skills, but a snippet of my journey and passion in this field. I love meeting people so feel free to connect with me!
